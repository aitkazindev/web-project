Campaign Overview

When the Medicube Booster Pro Mini was newly launched, a marketing campaign was conducted on Instagram in collaboration with a beauty and lifestyle influencer followed mostly by people aged 20–35. The newly introduced device, called "Mini," was promoted to young women and continues to sell well in the Mongolian market.
